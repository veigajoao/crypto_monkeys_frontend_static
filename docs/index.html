journey on the monkeys metaverse

Cryptomonkeys is a play-to-earn NFT game. Inspired on the movie “planet of the Apes”, monkeys in this game had their intelligence improved, to the point of conquering their own evolutionary world. NFTs span from pre historical monkeys all the way to a dystopian future.

play to earn

In this strategy game, players will use their monkeys to gain BNANAs (the token’s game) and gradually expand their time travelling monkey empire. BNANAs can then be sold on pancackeswap or used to mint more monkeys for your collection. The game consists of 3 different play to earn modes.

Your monkey NFT’s will be able to earn BNANAs by working in different work stations according to their intelligence level.

Is a tower defense game, in which you’ll be able to build and defend your monkey village using your NFTs and BNANA resources. The further you get into the game, the more BNANAs you’ll be able to win.

Is a Player versus player feature. you’ll be able to challenge other players to a battle betting your BNANAs, winner takes all. The goal is to destroy the other player’s village by attacking it with your monkey squad.

All our NFTs are authentic arts and fully designed by Juliano Carneiro, a renowned 3D artist and one of the founding members of our team.

The collection is inspired by historical evolution. Each expression, tesxture and style was designed to deliver the best possible experience for everyone.

Each monkey’s rarity depends on his chronological age, prehistorical monkeys are assigned a common rarity, the middle ages are rare, modern times epic and the futuristic apes are legendary.

The whole collection is BEP-721 compliant and hosted in Binance Smart Chain.
