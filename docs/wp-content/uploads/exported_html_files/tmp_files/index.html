Cryptomonkeys is a play-to-earn NFT game. Inspired on the movie “planet of the Apes”, monkeys in this game had their intelligence improved, to the point of conquering their own evolutionary world. NFTs span from pre historical monkeys all the way to a dystopian future.

In this strategy game, players will use their monkeys to gain BNANAs (the token’s game) and gradually expand their time travelling monkey empire. BNANAs can then be sold on pancackeswap or used to mint more monkeys for your collection. The game consists of 3 different play to earn modes.

Your monkey NFT’s will be able to earn BNANAs by working in different work stations according to their intelligence level.

A tower defense game, in which you’ll be able to build and defend your monkey village using your NFTs and BNANA resources. The further you get into the game, the more BNANAs you’ll be able to win.

 

A Player versus player feature. you’ll be able to challenge other players to a battle betting your BNANAs, winner takes all. The goal is to destroy the other player’s village by attacking it with your monkey squad.

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

All our NFTs are authentic arts and fully designed by Juliano Carneiro, a renowned 3D artist and one of the founding members of our team.

The collection is inspired by historical evolution. Each expression, tesxture and style was designed to deliver the best possible experience for everyone.

Each monkey’s rarity depends on his chronological age, prehistorical monkeys are assigned a common rarity, the middle ages are rare, modern times epic and the futuristic apes are legendary.

The whole collection is BEP-721 compliant and hosted in Binance Smart Chain.

Cryptomonkeys is a Play to Earn game in the blockchain.

To play the game you need to buy the $BNANA token, which is the exclusive game asset. Once you purchased the tokens, you can use them to buy a portal (read more about portals here) which will get you an exclusive NFT character.

All of our game modes require you to play using your NFT characters. In farm mode, you’ll use your NFTs to farm $BNANA tokens at different production sites, you’ll later be able to use those $BNANA tokens to mint more NFTS.

In strategy mode, you’ll defend your monkey village against invaders using your NFTs as your army in an exclusive Unreal Engine game. The longer your village last in the game, the bigger your $BNANA award at the end.

In PVP mode you’ll challenge other similar Cryptomonkeys players to a battle of villages in an Age-of-Empires inspired game. Each player will stake an amount of $BNANA and the winner takes all.

At anytime after the game launch you’ll be able to buy and sell your $BNANA tokens on PancackeSwap and other decentralized exchanges at market prices. You’ll also be able to stake your $BNANA in the monkey bank to earn interest.
